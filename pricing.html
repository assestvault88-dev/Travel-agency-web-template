Himalayan Base Camp

Nepal • 14 Days • High Difficulty

Overview

Embark on the adventure of a lifetime to the base of the world's highest peak. This 14-day trek takes you through sherpa villages, ancient monasteries, and breathtaking alpine scenery. Experience the unique culture of the Khumbu region and stand where legends have stood.

Max Altitude

5,364m

Group Size

Max 12

Meals

All Inclusive

Itinerary

Day 1-2: Arrival in Kathmandu

Arrival, transfer to hotel, and welcome dinner. Sightseeing in Kathmandu Valley.

Day 3: Fly to Lukla & Trek onto Phakding

Scenic flight to Tenzing-Hillary Airport. Start trek to Phakding (2,652m).

Day 4-6: Namche Bazaar & Acclimatization

Trek to the Sherpa capital. Rest day for acclimatization and exploration.

Day 7-13: Base Camp & Descent

Reach Everest Base Camp (5,364m) via Gorakshep. Begin descent back to Lukla.

Day 14: Fly to Kathmandu

Morning flight back to Kathmandu. Farewell dinner.

Inclusions

  • Airport transfers
  • 3 Nights hotel in Kathmandu
  • All teahouse accommodation during trek
  • Licensed English-speaking guide
  • Domestic flights (Kathmandu-Lukla)
  • Permits and Park Fees
